About Us

Company Profile

75 years of Independent India and more than 82 years of the Hari Prasad Gopi Krishna Saraf Group, resulted in the formation of its evolved identity AISSHPRA GEMS & JEWELS. The fundamental existence has been revamped to cater to the needs of the newer audience in and around Uttar Pradesh.

The new logo monogram of the Group is called “Swarna Kamal” (Golden Lotus) with its 8 petals in flowing form, it is symbolic of the purity of gold and the beauty of design which is what attracts every woman to her jewellery.

Root of Our Company

Aisshpra Gems and Jewels are 3rd Generation Jewellers, established in 1940. For the last 82 years the Group have been providing exceptional jewellery pieces and unmatched service to our customers.

TRANSFORMATION (PARIVARTAN) - milestones in the past 6 months:
The jewellery at Aisshpra is largely for the masses with gold, diamonds and gemstones dominating the entire range of inventory. There is also a fair amount of silver jewellery and artefacts and soon Aisshpra is also starting a bit of fashion jewellery in response to the needs of the local consumers.

Gorakhpur and the surrounding areas largely have an affinity for a buying pattern of 23/18 K Gold Purity. Inspite of the a few branded players having marked their foray, not much had changed in this regards. Aisshpra launched its forthright campaign “Parivartan ki aur hamara Kadam” that educated the local masses that Hallmarking needs to be embraced before it becomes a mandate. The call of 100% Hallmarking = 100% Buyback with an option of 18K Hallmarked Gold Jewellery received appreciation and acceptance by their customers and a big mind-gap was bridged for the industry as a whole. It also positions Aisshpra as a Brand of Trust, Integrity & Ethics and the regions premier jewellery player that talks the language of the industry.

Founders

Sri Balkrishnaji along with his elder brother Late Sri Gopi Krishnaji founded the store measuring only 100 square feet in the year 1940 for the sales of “zari and gota”. Expanding into the retail segment, they added silver articles like anklets and toe-rings to their collection. Slowly with increasing demand and customer confidence gold jewellery was added to the collection and the building in which the shop was rented was purchased for expansion purposes. Balkrishna ji while training his son’s Atul & Anoop always inculcated the core value’s of trust, integrity and ethics and asked them to make it a part of their daily life.

Mr. Atul Saraf who now helms all initiatives of the Hari Prasad Gopi Krishna group said “Times are changing far quicker than we think. Our consumers are evolving quicker than most of us retailers in Gorakhpur. In keeping with the requirements of the business and of the changing times, myself and Anoopji are thrilled to develop this new corporate identity AISSHPRA for our future successors to take it to greater heights in jewellery retailing".

Sri Balkrishnaji, the Patriarch of the Group, makes it a point to be present at the pilot store ensuring Customer Service while his grandsons, Mr. Saumitra and Mr. Vaibhav, the forerunners are taking charge of every other area of retail. Mrs. Vandana Saraf, their mother being the anchor woman of the house, looks after the merchandising and marketing functions with great participation. Aisshpra is truly a family managed business that is now embracing transformation to develop into an Organized Jewellery Retail Structure.

2) Manufacturing Unit:
Established in 2007 with thought of backward integration a diamond jewellery manufacturing unit with 15 employees was established in Gorakhpur and to facilitate correct procurement of cut and polished diamonds an office was set up in Opera House, Mumbai.
With a fast track growth we have now over 50 employees and the latest machinery, CAD software and CAM machine which enables us to design and produce world class jewellery.
