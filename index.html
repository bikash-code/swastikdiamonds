About Us

Aisshpra jewels is best jewellery shop in Jaunpur. 75 years of Independent India and 82 years of the Hari Prasad Gopi Krishna Saraf Group, resulted in the formation of its evolved identity Swastik Diamond - Brilliant & Beautiful. The fundamental existence has been revamped to cater to the needs of the newer audience in and around Uttar Pradesh. We are most trusted jewellery brand in Uttar Pradesh.

The new logo monogram of the Group is called “Swarna Kamal” (Golden Lotus) with its 8 petals in flowing form, it is symbolic of the purity of gold and the beauty of design which is what attracts every woman to her jewellery.
